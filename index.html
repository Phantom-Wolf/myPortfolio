Kitchen to Plate

Kitchen to Plate is a web application that gives you recipes for meals. The main difference of this app from most other recipe web apps is that instead of adding things that you want to see, you exclude what you don't want. This makes it perfect for seeing only the recipes that consider a groups varying dietary needs and preferences.
